Do you want to drill a borehole? Look no further. We are leaders in borehole drilling services

Geophysical Surevey.

This is the first stage in the borehole drilling process. Geophysical surveys helps delineate ground water availability, and it’s table level. At this point, we also determine the depth to be drilled.

drilling Starts

We then move to the site with the information from the geophysical survey. We high technical expertise in executing the drilling, and this is for both industrial and community purposes.

Pump installation

Our pump technicians have expertise ranging from the installation of submersible pumps, to the setup of complex constant pressure systems. We’ll get you the right system, and install it.

water test analysis

It is advisable that Water test analysis must be done at least once a year because foreign materials could drip into your borehole. We provide water test analysis for old and new boreholes.

If your borehole is giving you problems, don’t worry, we are there for you. With our experienced engineers, we can fix it in no time. It does not matter how old is the borehole.

Water Treatment (including IRON and SALT)
